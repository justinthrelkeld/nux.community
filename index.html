Middle Tennessee's community for user experience design. Together we:

  • Support, promote and teach UX, including experience strategy, content strategy, information architecture, interaction design, user interface, and usability.
  • Advocate for the value of UX to customers, business, developers and other audiences.
  • Foster professional networks through social and educational events.

We meet on the last Thursday of the month and most events are free to attend.
